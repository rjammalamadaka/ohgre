How It Works

  • Customers will now earn TWO miles for every dollar spent on their ONG supplier natural gas charges (excluding sales tax and one-time charges)!
  • And, if you are a Delta SkyMiles® Medallion® member, Delta will award an additional mile for each dollar spent as described above!
  • It’s easy to start participating. Simply enter your ONG account and SkyMiles member information below. If you’d prefer to enroll by phone, call ONG (toll-free) at 1-888-466-4427 and mention that you would like to enroll in ONG’s Delta SkyMiles partnership program. Please have your SkyMiles account number handy when you call.
